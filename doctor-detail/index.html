Research Focus

My current research is focused on optimizing patients' outcomes in skull base surgery, sinonasal oncology and inflammatory diseases of the sinuses. I have contributed to more than 18 peer-reviewed articles, co-edited a textbook on sinonasal disorders, authored and co-authored book chapters published in six different textbooks, presented more than 20 oral presentations and performed live cadaveric sinonasal and skull base dissections at national and international meetings.
